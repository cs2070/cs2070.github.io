WORK ABOUT RESUME

SAVE THE WHALES

Redesigning the nonprofit website

Project Overview

Project Description: Save the Whales is developing a better donate feature and more ways to educate and share ways to help the cause to help people who want to conserve marine life to learn more about how to make an impact in their homes or by donating.

Website: www.savethewhales.org

Team Members: Christine Shin, Samantha Yee, Angie Romona Torres, Isaac DongJun Lee

My Role: User Research, Wireframe, UI Style Guide, Prototype

Tools Used: Adobe XD, Miro, Storyboardthat, Zoom, Photoshop, Flowmapp, Trello, Google Drive

Timeline: 3 weeks

01. Discovery


Proto Persona


To understand our user, we first created Proto Persona.

Everyone meet Olive! A graphic Designer living in Southern California. Olive has just watched the documentary Blackfish on Netflix with her family and is feeling a little sad about the current state of marine life. She wants to learn more about what she can do with her kids while in quarentine to help the situation so she goes to her computer and opens save the whales.org

Survey


We researched peopleâ€™s awareness of environmental issues, level of interest in nonprofit organizations, and factors that influence their donation decision on nonprofit organizations. The survey was created using a google form, and the interview was done either online or in-person.

40 people participated in survey. The survey result show that 92.5% of the participants think the environment is in a bad state. It also indicates that the mission statement and personal connection to the issue are two of the most important factors that influence the donation decision. According to the interview data, the trustworthiness of the organization most likely affects the donation decision.

Interview


We interviewed 5 people. Most of the interviewee said that the trustworthiness of the organization most likely affects the donation decision.
